Challenges and Hackathons

Smart City Research Center at IIIT Hyderabad is planning to conduct various Challenges and Hackathons to bring new solutions and technologies to our rapidly changing world by making our Living Lab handy as a test bed to get the ideas and technologies blend.

In this context, the centre invite proposals for challenges from organizations/ individuals having similar interests.

The Challenge proposals are invited for any challenge involving IoT / AI based solutions in the following areas but not limited to

  • Environmental Quality Monitoring (Air Quality & Weather Monitoring)
  • Water Quality and Quantity
  • Conventional & Renewable Power

Water Challenge for Startups

IIIT Hyderabad is looking for non-invasive methods to measure water quality and quantity . Start-ups with solution can register here. Shortlisted start-ups will demonstrate the solution in POC at IIIT Hyderabad Campus. Winners will get a pilot from IIIT Hyderabad.

    Eligibility Critetria:
  • Applying team should be a startup
  • Measuring method should be non-invasive
    Prizes:
  • IIIT Hyderabad will provide a launch oppurtunity
