Smart Campus

The smart campus is a live setup, all wired to support the various areas like pollution, weather, water, smart spaces etc. At smart campus, we are working on several interesting IoT projects in the areas of pollution, energy, water, smart lampposts, crowd monitoring, smart classrooms, and dashboards. It is essentially a live setup of a building, campus or facility that gets used for its intended purposes along with being a ‘living’ test bed for research and innovation.
