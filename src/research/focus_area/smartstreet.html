Smart Street Light

Why is it important?

Street lights consume a large amount of energy and there are a lot of street light which we would be difficult to keep track when one breaks.

What are we trying to do?

With smart street lamps we conserve electricity by deeming the lights when street is empty inform the authority with the location if the bulb goes bad and with inbuilt cameras we can increase the security for the people
